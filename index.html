Welcome to the MDB-SDG Dashboard.

This dashboard collects all operations by nine major Multilateral Development Banks (MDBs) from 2016 to 2025. Through natural language processing of project descriptions, a fine-tuned BERT model classifies all operations into the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The results capture general trends in MDBs' efforts to "finance a sustainable future" over this decade.
Starting from the chart below, hover over the bars to see how many projects and the total amount approved by MDBs for a particular year and SDG. You can also select an MDB from the left panel to filter and view MDB-specific results. Ready to dive deeper? Try MDB Finance by SDG for goal-level insights, or Explore by Country for geographic perspectives and country-level stats. Enjoy exploring!
